General

How long will it take to setup my new controller?

We typically setup new controllers within 2 hours of the order taking place. This can vary though depending on payment checks and availability of resource. We would always aim to complete setup within 24 hours.

Where will my controller be located?

You can choose your preferred location when placing an order. We currently provide controllers in London UK, Atlanta US, Chicago US, Seattle US, Toronto CA, Paris FR, Stockholm SE, Sydney AU, Tokyo JP and Singapore SG.

Will you handle any UniFi software updates?

Once we have completed testing on the latest stable software release, we will update your controller. Typically, this release testing phase lasts between 1 to 2 weeks. However, if a software release fails to meet our stability standards, we may skip it. As for device firmware updates, it is your responsibility to manage these through the web interface.

If you would prefer to receieve updates more quickly just let us know and we can deploy them to your systems as soon as they become available from UniFi.

Can I use my own custom domain name?

Yes, just drop us an e-mail and we will send you instructions on how to set this up.

Will I receive SSH access to my controller?

In order to ensure the security and reliability of your controller, we do not provide you with SSH access at this moment in time.

Can you restrict my controller to only allow access from my IP address?

Of course. Just drop us an e-mail and we will configure our firewall to only allow access from your IP address.

Can I change the location of my controller?

We can do this for you but it will mean your controller IP address will change. Drop us an e-mail with the location you'd like your controller to be migrated to.

How often do you backup my controller?

We backup your controller every night and you can request a restore by dropping us an e-mail anytime.

Billing

How easy is it to cancel my controller?

Very easy. We don't ask our customers to commit to any minimum contract terms. You can cancel your controller with just 30 days' notice. Please e-mail us to request a cancellation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Do I need to be a business to sign up?

Certainly not. We are happy for both businesses and individuals to sign up to our services.

Are there any setup or hidden fees?

Absolutely not. We do not charge any setup fees and the price you see is the price you pay. You can also cancel with just 30 days' notice.

Can I get a discount for paying annually?

This isn't currently possible. However, we are considering introducing an annual payment option in future.

Which currency do you charge in?

We are a UK based company aiming to market our services globally. We currently only charge for services in USD but we are looking at introducing different currencies in the future.

Do you offer discounts to charities and non-profits?

Yes, we'd be happy to offer a discount. Please drop us an e-mail to learn more.

How secure is my payment information?

All payments are handled by Stripe. We do not store your card details within our systems.
