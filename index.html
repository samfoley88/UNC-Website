UniFiCentral Features

A UniFiCentral Controller offers identical functionalities to that of an on-premise controller, such as CloudKey or UDM PLUS:

24/7 Monitoring

Our team provides continuous monitoring of your UniFiCentral Controller, ensuring its availability and security every hour of every day, all year round.

Streamlined Management

With a UniFiCentral Controller, you can manage an unlimited number of sites, streamlining your network management and reducing hardware costs.

Updates & Backups

Your UniFiCentral Controller is automatically updated, and a complete Controller backup is taken every evening for both your protection and peace of mind.

Multi-Platform Support

You can access your UniFiCentral Controller through the web or Ubiquiti UniFi mobile apps (iOS & Android).
